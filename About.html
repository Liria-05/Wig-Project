Our Story

Welcome to Mayvenn Wigs

Settled in the beautiful neighborhood of West Hollywood, just squares from Beverly Hills, our rich space is truly a reflection of both Andy and Leanne's trend-setting style; an inviting and hip environment that inspires and accommodates its select clientele.

The open organization has a moderate, industrial design that is distinctly unique and modern. The world of wigs is an ever-changing landscape of human hair and artificial adornments that can be worn for cosmetic, balding, or hair preservation purposes.

It's no wonder we're constantly searching for the best materials to build our collection. Today's wigs have transformed so much that most can't even tell the difference between an actual scalp and a wig scalp.
